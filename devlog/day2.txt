 The snail doesn't just chase your cursor position — it pathfinds along the actual stroke trail you've drawn on the canvas, using your lines like roads. Even after you lift your mouse, the snail is crawling along your previous strokes toward wherever you are now. Your own drawing becomes the thing hunting you.
